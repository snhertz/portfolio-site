Proof of Methodology

This framework isn't theoretical. I built this exact model as a paid assessment for a real company — and it's how I approach every growth problem.

Paid Hiring Assessment

Top Nutrition Coaching — Growth Calculator

A DTC nutrition coaching company asked me to build a comprehensive growth model as part of their hiring process. I delivered a 10-tab interactive calculator in Google Sheets covering:

  • Multi-channel funnel analysis across paid social, organic, partnerships, and referral programs
  • Week-by-week revenue projections with compounding customer growth and churn modeling
  • ICE Framework prioritization to rank 12+ growth initiatives by impact, confidence, and ease
  • Scenario modeling (conservative / base / aggressive) with adjustable assumptions
  • Unit economics dashboard calculating CAC, LTV, payback period, and ROAS per channel

The methodology works because it forces clarity:

Structured Prioritization
ICE scoring removes gut-feel from channel selection — every initiative competes on the same scale
Transparent Assumptions
Every input is visible and adjustable, so stakeholders can challenge numbers instead of guessing
Week-Level Granularity
Monthly models hide too much. Weekly projections catch problems fast and show real growth curves

What I'd Do in 90 Days

Here's how I'd take this from model to reality — a phased approach that builds momentum while maintaining analytical rigor at every step.

01
Days 1–30

Foundation & First Tests

  • Audit existing acquisition data and attribution setup — understand what's measurable today
  • Instrument end-to-end tracking (UTMs, conversion API, pixel setup) for Meta, TikTok, and landing pages
  • Launch first Meta test campaigns: 3-5 ad sets targeting family/senior segments with varied creative angles
  • Build the creative testing framework: hypothesis format, naming conventions, performance thresholds, kill criteria
  • Establish baseline CAC and conversion benchmarks from initial campaigns to calibrate the model
02
Days 31–60

Scale Winners & Expand Channels

  • Double down on top-performing Meta ad sets — scale spend on proven creative while maintaining ROAS targets
  • Launch TikTok test campaigns: UGC-style creative, product demos, and customer testimonial formats
  • Stand up the creative production pipeline — weekly output cadence with structured testing cycles
  • Begin influencer outreach to 20-30 family/tech review creators, negotiate initial partnership terms
  • Refine landing pages based on channel-specific conversion data — separate pages per traffic source if needed
03
Days 61–90

Optimize Toward $100K MRR Target

  • Kill underperformers decisively — reallocate budget from low-ROAS channels to proven winners
  • Scale proven channels aggressively toward the $100K MRR target, monitoring for diminishing returns
  • Launch first influencer campaigns and measure initial CAC against paid channel benchmarks
  • Build a data-driven scaling plan for months 4-6 with updated unit economics from real campaign data
  • Present comprehensive performance review: what worked, what didn't, and the path to scaling further
