The Framework

Before spending a dollar, I prioritize initiatives using the ICE framework: Impact, Confidence, and Ease. Proof of Methodology

I built this model for Top Nutrition Coaching as a freelance project and have used the framework at every company since. The goal: set clear kill criteria, test fast, and know within weeks whether a channel scales or gets cut.

Top Nutrition Coaching Growth Calculator

Top Nutrition Coaching paid me to build a comprehensive growth model for their business. The framework worked so well that it became the foundation for how I approach pipeline metrics at every company since. Using Claude Code, I adapted the original spreadsheet into this interactive version. The 10-tab calculator in Google Sheets covered:

  • Multi-channel funnel analysis across paid social, organic, partnerships, and referral programs
  • Week-by-week revenue projections with compounding customer growth and churn modeling
  • ICE Framework prioritization to rank 12+ growth initiatives by impact, confidence, and ease
  • Scenario modeling (conservative / base / aggressive) with adjustable assumptions
  • Unit economics dashboard calculating CAC, LTV, payback period, and ROAS per channel

Structured Prioritization

Week-Level Granularity
Monthly models hide too much. Weekly projections catch problems fast and show real growth curves

The First 90 Days

The target is $100K net-new ARR by month 6. Here's how I'd spend the first half: testing fast, killing bad ideas decisively, and building the systems that let proven channels scale.

01
Days 1–30

Meta First, Build the Foundation

  • Audit existing acquisition data and attribution setup to understand what's measurable today
  • Instrument end-to-end tracking (UTMs, conversion API, pixel setup) for Meta and landing pages
  • Launch Meta test campaigns: 3-5 ad sets targeting family/senior segments with varied creative angles
  • Build the creative testing framework: hypothesis format, naming conventions, performance thresholds, kill criteria
  • Build channel-specific landing pages and start optimizing against real conversion data
  • Establish baseline CAC and conversion benchmarks from Meta to calibrate the growth model
02
Days 31–60

Scale Meta, Open Second Channels

  • Scale top-performing Meta ad sets, increasing spend on proven creative while watching for diminishing returns
  • Stand up the creative production pipeline with a weekly output cadence and structured testing cycles
  • Begin influencer outreach to 20-30 family/tech review creators, negotiate initial partnership terms and set up tracking
  • Launch YouTube test campaigns targeting high-intent keywords and audiences, measuring direct CAC/LTV performance
  • Refine landing pages based on Meta data, build separate pages for influencer and YouTube traffic
  • Optionally test CTV/OTT as a top-of-funnel awareness play if early channels validate unit economics
03
Days 61–90

Validate Second Channels, Plan the Scale

  • Measure initial influencer and YouTube results against Meta's proven CAC benchmarks
  • Kill underperformers decisively, reallocate budget to whatever is working
  • Check lead quality across all channels against actual downstream conversion, not just top-of-funnel volume
  • If CTV is running, test whether awareness spend improves Meta and direct mail conversion rates (multi-channel lift)
  • Build a data-driven scaling plan for months 4-6 with real unit economics, targeting $100K net-new ARR by month 6
